How I find astronomy interesting:


Why astronomy is theoretically fascinating:

  • It can lead to humanity discovering if life can be sustained independent of earth.

  • It can lead to discovery of useful materials on other planets

  • The pursuit of astronomical knowledge usually leads to the discovery technology that is useful in other fields.

Examples of how it's pragamtic:

  • A method developed by radio astronomers is now used as a non-invasive way to detect tumours.

  • X-ray observatory technology is also used in current X-ray luggage belts in airports.

  • Observations of stellar distributions on the sky ,which are used to point and calibrate telescopes, are also used in aerospace engineering.
